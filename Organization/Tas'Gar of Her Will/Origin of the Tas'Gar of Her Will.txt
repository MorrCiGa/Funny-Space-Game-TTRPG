Before the detonation Tas’Gar was fragmented into seven distinct and militaristic factions, which are detailed in the culture section, but prior to the detonation a being from beyond the mortal veil visited them, she told them that if the sentient races are to continue they need to work together, under one flag following a specific doctrine which she laid out, after this message the seven factions unified and under her will, shortly after detonation, around 2AD.

Tas’Gar is a police state, prioritizing surveillance and imprisonment, along with r-education and other means of populous control as to most closely follow out the doctrine she told them
